About Me

My name is Monica Nadal. I live and work in Los Angeles, California. I have my bachelors from the University of California--Irvine in Film & Media Studies and Political Science. I am a media technology professional, specializing in finding creative technical solutions for clients and for b2b organizations.

As a consumer of different technologies and media, and with a background in marketing, media engineering, and as an avid admirer of art and design, I believe in interactions with our tools should not only be functional, but intuitive and aesthetically pleasing.

In terms of professional interests, I am fascinated by web design, solutions architecture, media engineering, UI/UX, and legal implications of intellectual property and privacy. My personal interests and hobbies include writing, photography, reading (fiction, non-fiction, the backs of cereal boxes), being quietly inappropriate at art museums, and general pretentious a$$hat stuff.
